**Analysis Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Target Role:** £50k+ hybrid developer roles in UK Midlands  
**Current Role:** £37k with 3 years experience  

## Executive Summary
Tom's portfolio is **technically impressive but commercially weak**. He has real depth in AI integration and full-stack development, but the presentation doesn't match what actually gets people hired at £50k+ levels. The hero section feels like buzzword stuffing, case studies lack business impact, and the overall narrative doesn't build confidence for a £13k salary jump.
